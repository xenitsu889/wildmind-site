COMMON CHALLENGES

What we hear from logistics clients.

Before we propose anything, we listen. These are the patterns we see repeatedly across the sector, and the problems our work tends to solve.

01

Manual dispatch

Dispatchers assign routes over phone and WhatsApp, with no central view of capacity, driver availability, or delivery priorities.

02

No real-time visibility

Operations teams can't see where vehicles are, which deliveries are delayed, or what's blocking the next run until customers call to complain.

03

Excel-based tracking

Fleet status, fuel logs, maintenance schedules, and delivery records live in disconnected spreadsheets, impossible to audit or scale.

04

No route intelligence

Routes are planned from memory and habit, not data. Fuel costs, idle time, and missed delivery windows add up silently every week.
