01 — Problem

Operations running on spreadsheets and memory.

Aditya Chemicals — a specialty chemicals manufacturer — was managing their entire operation through a patchwork of Excel files, WhatsApp messages, and manual records. Order tracking, inventory levels, dispatch scheduling, and invoicing all lived in disconnected systems with no single source of truth.

Management had no real-time visibility into operations. Errors in order fulfillment were only discovered after dispatch. Inventory discrepancies were caught at month-end. The business was growing, but the operational infrastructure couldn't scale with it.

02 — Solution

A single digital backbone for the entire operation.

We built a custom web-based operations platform that centralizes every workflow — from order intake to dispatch confirmation — into a single system with role-based access, real-time dashboards, and automated reporting. The platform was designed to match the team's existing mental model of the business, minimizing the learning curve while dramatically improving operational clarity.

03b — Quality Management

From paper files to a controlled digital quality system.

Before the QMS module, Aditya Chemicals managed their entire quality documentation process manually — physical files, printed SOPs, handwritten sign-offs, and paper certificates stored in folders across departments. Retrieving a specific document version meant physically searching through files. Ensuring staff were working from the latest approved SOP had no reliable mechanism. Certificate expiry was tracked on spreadsheets or not at all.

We replaced the entire paper-based process with a digital document control system built directly into the existing operations platform — so quality management sits alongside order management, inventory, and dispatch in one unified system.

/01

Document Control

Every quality document — SOPs, work instructions, quality certificates (CoA), safety data sheets (SDS/MSDS), and batch records — stored, versioned, and controlled in a single system. No more physical files, no more version confusion.

/02

Version Control

Every document revision is tracked with a full history. When a SOP is updated, the previous version is archived and the new version becomes active automatically after approval. Teams always work from the current approved version — enforced by the system, not by memory.

/03

Approval Workflows

Documents go through a defined approval chain before they become active. Quality managers review and sign off digitally. No document reaches the floor without authorised approval — replacing the informal and inconsistent paper sign-off process.

/04

Employee Acknowledgement

Once a document is approved and published, relevant team members are notified and required to confirm they have read and understood it. Acknowledgements are logged with timestamp and user — creating a verifiable record that staff were informed of procedure changes.

/05

Expiry Alerts

Certificates, compliance documents, and time-sensitive records trigger automated alerts before expiry. Management knows in advance when documents need renewal — eliminating the risk of operating with expired certificates that previously went unnoticed until audit time.

/06

Instant Search & Retrieval

Any document, any version, any date — searchable and retrievable in seconds. What previously required physically locating a physical file now takes a single search query. Particularly critical during customer audits and compliance reviews.
