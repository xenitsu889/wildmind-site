01 — Problem

Operations running on spreadsheets and memory.

Aditya Chemicals — a specialty chemicals manufacturer — was managing their entire operation through a patchwork of Excel files, WhatsApp messages, and manual records. Order tracking, inventory levels, dispatch scheduling, and invoicing all lived in disconnected systems with no single source of truth.

Management had no real-time visibility into operations. Errors in order fulfillment were only discovered after dispatch. Inventory discrepancies were caught at month-end. The business was growing, but the operational infrastructure couldn't scale with it.

02 — Solution

A single digital backbone for the entire operation.

We built a custom web-based operations platform that centralizes every workflow — from order intake to dispatch confirmation — into a single system with role-based access, real-time dashboards, and automated reporting. The platform was designed to match the team's existing mental model of the business, minimizing the learning curve while dramatically improving operational clarity.

03 — Features

What we built.

/01

Order Management

End-to-end order lifecycle — from intake and confirmation through production scheduling, dispatch, and delivery tracking — with full audit history.

/02

Inventory Control

Real-time inventory tracking across raw materials and finished goods, with low-stock alerts, batch tracking, and automated reorder suggestions.

/03

Dispatch & Logistics

Dispatch scheduling, vehicle assignment, delivery confirmation, and proof-of-delivery capture — all in one workflow with driver-facing mobile views.

/04

Invoicing & Billing

Automated invoice generation from confirmed orders, with GST compliance, payment tracking, and outstanding balance reporting.

/05

Management Dashboards

Real-time operational dashboards giving management instant visibility into order status, inventory levels, dispatch performance, and revenue metrics.

/06

Role-based Access Control

Granular permissions for management, operations, warehouse, dispatch, and accounts teams — each seeing only what they need to do their job.

04 — Architecture

Reliable, maintainable, and built to grow.

Built on a Next.js frontend with a Node.js API layer, Firebase for real-time data sync and authentication, and PostgreSQL for transactional data. The architecture prioritizes reliability and data integrity — critical for a manufacturing operation where errors have real downstream costs.
