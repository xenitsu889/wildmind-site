ENGAGEMENT MODELS

Flexible ways to work together.

Choose the model that fits your timeline, budget, and internal structure. All engagements include a dedicated point of contact and weekly progress reporting.

Dedicated Developer

One senior engineer embedded in your team full-time. They attend your standups, use your tools, and report directly to your tech lead. Best for sustained product development over 3+ months.

Team Augmentation

Two to four engineers working alongside your existing team on specific modules or sprints. Ideal when you need capacity for a feature push without a long-term hire.

Project Squad

A self-contained mini-team — full-stack, backend, and DevOps — that delivers a scoped project end-to-end. Fixed timeline, fixed scope, weekly demos.
