How it works

From legacy risk to modern, maintainable system.

01 Discovery

Audit the legacy system

Map current architecture, business logic, and the real risk of each component staying or moving.

02 Design

Plan the modernization path

Sequence components for replacement based on business risk, technical debt, and dependency order.

03 Build

Modernize incrementally

Components replaced and validated one at a time, with weekly demos against real workflows.

04 Launch

Decommission the old system

Legacy components retired only once the replacement has proven itself in production.
